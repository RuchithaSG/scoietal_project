Our Vision & Mission

At Jeevan Jyothi Residential School, we believe in holistic education that fosters intellectual, emotional, and moral growth. Our goal is to create a safe and nurturing environment where students can excel academically while developing strong values of compassion, responsibility, and leadership.

Our Values

  • Excellence
  • Inclusivity
  • Discipline & Integrity
  • Community Engagement

Special Programs

We run a dedicated deaf school, ensuring accessibility through:

  • Qualified sign language instructors
  • Custom-tailored learning materials
  • Specialized technology for communication
  • Speech therapy & audiology support
