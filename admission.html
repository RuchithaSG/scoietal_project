Admission Process

We welcome students from all backgrounds to join our vibrant and inclusive learning community.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Fill out the application form.
  2. Submit the required documents (birth certificate, previous academic records, etc.).
  3. Attend an admission interview.
  4. Receive confirmation and pay the admission fee.
  5. Join the orientation program and begin your journey with us!

Required Documents:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Academic Records
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Medical Certificate
  • Passport Size Photos (3 copies)

Why Choose Us?

Jeevan Jyothi Residential School offers a world-class education with modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a focus on holistic development.

  • Experienced Faculty: Our dedicated and highly qualified teachers ensure that each student receives personal attention.
  • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Well-equipped classrooms, smart boards, and modern labs enhance the learning experience.
  • Inclusive Education: We provide special support for hearing-impaired students, ensuring equal opportunities for all.
  • Extracurricular Activities: Students can engage in various sports, music, dance, and arts programs to develop their talents.
  • Safe & Secure Campus: 24/7 security, CCTV surveillance, and a well-maintained hostel provide a secure environment for students.
  • Strong Academic Foundation: Our curriculum is designed to build problem-solving skills, creativity, and leadership qualities.
  • Focus on Moral Values: We instill discipline, integrity, and social responsibility in our students.
  • Career Guidance & Counseling: We help students make informed career choices and prepare for higher education.

Join us today and become a part of a school that not only educates but also nurtures every child to achieve their full potential.
