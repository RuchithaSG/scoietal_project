Our Special Deaf School Program

Jeevan Jyothi Residential School is committed to providing inclusive education. Our dedicated deaf school offers:

  • Qualified sign language instructors
  • Assistive learning technologies & smart classrooms
  • Custom curriculum tailored for hearing-impaired students
  • Speech therapy & audiology support
  • Interactive learning through visual aids
